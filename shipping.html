Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your Ocassion 365 pieces. If something is not right, we are here to help.

Return Policy

  • Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with all tags attached
  • Buyer responsible for return shipping costs
  • Refund processed within 3–5 business days after we receive the item

Non-Returnable Items

  • Sale or discounted items (final sale)
  • Items showing signs of wear or use
  • Items returned without original packaging

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp or email within 7 days of receiving your order
  2. Include your order number and reason for return
  3. We will provide return instructions and address
  4. Ship the item back to us
  5. Once received and inspected, we will process your refund

Exchanges

For size exchanges, please initiate a return and place a new order for the correct size. This ensures the fastest processing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship outside Kenya? ▼
Currently, we only ship within Kenya. We hope to expand to East Africa and beyond soon.
Can I change or cancel my order? ▼
Orders can be changed or cancelled within 2 hours of placing them. Please contact us on WhatsApp immediately if you need to make changes.
What if my item arrives damaged? ▼
Please take photos of the damaged item and packaging and contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a replacement or refund.
How do I know my size? ▼
Each product page includes size guidance. For belts, we recommend measuring your waist where you intend to wear the belt. For sneakers, we fit true to standard Kenyan sizes. Contact us on WhatsApp for personalized sizing help.
Do you offer wholesale or bulk orders? ▼
Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for boutiques and stylists. Please email hello@ocassion365.com with "Wholesale Inquiry" in the subject line.
